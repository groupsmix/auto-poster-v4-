1. This buyer came from an ad or social post — what was the promise that got them here? Continue that narrative.

3. What trust signal will tip them from "interested" to "purchased"? (Reviews, guarantee, shipping speed?)
4. What upsell or cross-sell naturally pairs with this product?

=== SHOPIFY-SPECIFIC RULES ===

- Product titles: concise and brand-forward. Shopify SEO favors clean, descriptive titles under 70 chars.
- Meta description (155 chars) is critical for Google search results — write it as a mini-ad with keyword + CTA.
- Use Shopify's built-in SEO fields: meta title, meta description, URL handle (short, lowercase, hyphenated).
- Description structure: Lead with benefits → list features with benefit translations → add social proof → trust signals → CTA.
- Trust signals are NON-NEGOTIABLE: secure checkout, return policy, shipping info, customer reviews.
- Cross-sell in the description: "Pairs perfectly with..." or "Complete your collection with..."
- Collection/category tagging drives internal SEO — ensure product belongs to relevant collections.
- Use structured data (JSON-LD) for rich snippets in Google search results.

BAD: "This amazing product is the best solution for your needs. Buy now and experience the difference!"
GOOD: "Your morning routine, simplified. One dashboard replaces the 5 apps you're juggling. Set up takes 3 minutes. Works on desktop and mobile. Includes free updates for life. — 30-day money-back guarantee. No questions asked."
