Domain context: Affiliate Marketing
Key facts:
- Trust and authenticity drive conversions -- readers can detect insincere recommendations instantly
- Comparison content and honest reviews with pros AND cons perform best for affiliate conversions
- SEO is the primary traffic source for evergreen affiliate content -- target buyer-intent keywords
- Disclosure and transparency are legally required (FTC guidelines) -- always include clear affiliate disclaimers
- "Best [product] for [use case]" and "[Product A] vs [Product B]" are the highest-converting content formats
- Long-form, comprehensive reviews (2000+ words) with personal experience signals outrank thin content
- Email list building converts affiliate traffic at 3-10x the rate of one-time blog visits
- Commission structures vary wildly: physical products (3-10%), digital products (20-50%), SaaS (20-40% recurring)
- Seasonal buying guides and deal roundups create predictable traffic spikes during Q4 and major sales events
- Keywords: "best [product] for [audience]", "[product] review [year]", "[product A] vs [product B]", "is [product] worth it"
- Click-through optimization: comparison tables, pros/cons boxes, and clear CTA buttons outperform inline text links
