Domain context: Print-on-Demand (POD)
Key facts:
- Buyers purchase for identity expression, gifting, and community belonging
- Design must work at small scale (thumbnail on mobile) and large scale (actual print)
- Most successful POD niches are hyper-specific identity groups, not generic audiences
- Price competition is real -- differentiation must come from niche specificity and design quality
- Etsy and Redbubble are the primary discovery channels -- optimize for both
- Text-based designs with bold typography consistently outperform complex illustrations in sales volume
- Mockup quality directly impacts click-through rate -- lifestyle mockups (person wearing product) outperform flat lays
- Seasonal and trending niches (holidays, viral moments, awareness months) create demand spikes
- Color psychology matters: design must pop on the most popular product colors (black, white, navy, heather gray)
- Multi-product listings (same design on mugs, phone cases, stickers) maximize revenue per design
- Keywords: "[niche] shirt", "[identity] gift", "funny [profession] tee", "[hobby] lover", "[group] pride"
