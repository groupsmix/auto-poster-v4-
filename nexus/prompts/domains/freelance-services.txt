Domain context: Freelance Services
Key facts:
- Clients buy outcomes and expertise, not hours -- position every service around the result delivered
- Portfolio and case studies with measurable results drive conversions better than credentials alone
- Pricing should reflect value delivered, not time spent -- package services into fixed-price deliverables
- Specialization in a niche commands 2-5x premium rates over generalist positioning
- Fiverr, Upwork, and direct outreach are primary channels with fundamentally different buyer expectations
- Response time and communication quality are top factors in client satisfaction and repeat business
- Testimonials with specific results ("increased revenue by 34%") outperform generic praise
- Service productization (fixed scope, fixed price, fixed timeline) scales better than custom proposals
- Upsells and retainers convert best when offered after initial delivery exceeds expectations
- Keywords: "[skill] freelancer", "hire [specialist]", "[service] agency", "[niche] consultant"
- Gig descriptions must address: deliverables, timeline, revisions included, what the client needs to provide
