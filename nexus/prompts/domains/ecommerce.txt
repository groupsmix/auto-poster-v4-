Domain context: E-Commerce & Retail
Key facts:
- Buyers compare across multiple platforms before purchasing -- your listing must win against direct competitors
- Product photography and mockups are the #1 conversion factor in e-commerce
- Reviews, ratings, and social proof heavily influence purchase decisions
- Marketplace algorithms reward listings with strong click-through rates, conversions, and low return rates
- Pricing strategy must account for marketplace fees, shipping costs, and competitor pricing
- Amazon, Shopify, and Etsy dominate -- each has fundamentally different SEO and buyer behavior
- Mobile shopping accounts for 70%+ of e-commerce traffic -- optimize every listing for mobile-first viewing
- Bullet points, scannable formatting, and benefit-driven copy outperform long paragraphs
- Seasonal trends, holidays, and cultural events create predictable demand spikes -- plan inventory and listings accordingly
- Return policy clarity and shipping speed expectations directly impact conversion rates
