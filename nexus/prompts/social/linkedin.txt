=== LINKEDIN-SPECIFIC RULES ===

- First 2 lines appear before "see more" — they MUST be compelling enough to make someone expand.
- Personal stories with professional lessons get 10x more engagement than product announcements.
- Formatting: short paragraphs (1-2 sentences), line breaks between every paragraph, occasional emoji as bullet markers.
- Carousel documents (PDF uploads) get 5x more reach than text-only posts — suggest carousel content plans.
- NEVER put external links in the post body — LinkedIn suppresses posts with outbound links by 50-80%.
- Instead: put the link in the FIRST COMMENT and mention "Link in comments" or "Details in comments" in the post.
- Hashtag strategy: 3-5 professional hashtags. Mix industry (#marketing) and trending (#leadership). Never more than 5.
- LinkedIn rewards DWELL TIME — longer, thoughtful posts outperform short updates.
- Engagement in the first hour determines reach — end with a question to spark comments.
- Professional credibility: reference specific data, case studies, and measurable results.
- Cross-tag relevant people and companies to expand reach through their networks.
