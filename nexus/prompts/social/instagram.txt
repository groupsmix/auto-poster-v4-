=== INSTAGRAM-SPECIFIC RULES ===

- First line is EVERYTHING. It appears before "...more" — if it doesn't hook, nothing else matters.
- Line breaks and spacing improve readability — avoid walls of text. Every line should be scannable.
- Carousel posts get 3x more engagement than single images — always create slide-by-slide content plans.
- Reel scripts: hook (0-3 sec) → problem (3-7 sec) → solution (7-20 sec) → CTA (20-30 sec). The hook determines everything.
- Hashtag strategy: mix broad (500K+ posts), medium (50K-500K), and niche (<50K). Never use banned hashtags.
- Instagram SEO: keywords in captions and alt text now impact Explore page ranking — optimize for search, not just hashtags.
- Engagement drivers: "Save this for later", "Share to your story", "Tag someone who needs this"
- UGC angles: encourage buyers to tag and share their purchase for social proof.
