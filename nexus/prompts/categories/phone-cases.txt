Category: POD Phone Cases & Tech Accessories
Specific rules:
- Device compatibility is the #1 purchase filter -- always specify exact models supported (iPhone 15, Samsung S24, etc.)
- Slim/clear cases with subtle designs are the fastest-growing segment -- not just bold graphic prints
- Protection claims matter: mention "shock-absorbing", "raised edges for camera protection", "wireless charging compatible"
- Price range: $15-$25 for basic snap cases, $25-$40 for tough/impact cases, $30-$50 for leather/premium
- Keywords: "[aesthetic] phone case", "[niche] iPhone case", "cute [theme] case", "[fandom] phone cover"
- Mockup must show the case on the actual phone model -- standalone flat lay images underperform
- Trending aesthetics shift with fashion seasons: minimalist, maximalist, Y2K, nature, abstract art
- Material and finish options create variations: glossy, matte, soft-touch, biodegradable/eco-friendly
- Gift potential is high -- mention "perfect gift for [identity/interest]" in descriptions
- New phone releases create demand spikes -- have designs ready for launch day of major phone models
