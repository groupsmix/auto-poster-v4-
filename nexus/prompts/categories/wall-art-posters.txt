Category: Wall Art & Posters (Digital + POD)
Specific rules:
- Two distinct markets: digital download prints (instant, buyer prints at home) and POD shipped prints
- Digital prints are pure margin -- no production cost, instant delivery, infinite inventory
- Price range: $3-$12 for digital downloads, $15-$45 for POD prints, $35-$120 for framed/canvas
- Size options are critical: offer 5x7, 8x10, 11x14, 16x20, 18x24 minimum for digital downloads
- Keywords: "printable wall art", "[aesthetic] poster", "[niche] print", "gallery wall set", "digital download art"
- Gallery wall sets (3-5 coordinated prints) command 2-3x the price of individual prints
- Color palette consistency matters: buyers want prints that match their existing room decor
- Trending styles: abstract, botanical, typography quotes, minimalist line art, vintage illustrations
- Room mockups showing the art on a wall in a styled room are essential for conversion
- File format matters for digital: include high-res PDF, JPG, and PNG for maximum printing flexibility
- Seasonal art (holiday, seasonal decor) creates predictable demand cycles -- list early for each season
