Specific rules:

- Professional formatting and design increase perceived value -- invest in layout, not just words
- Table of contents and chapter structure signal quality and help buyers assess value before purchase

- Include checklists, worksheets, and templates within the PDF to increase perceived and actual value
- Page count should be mentioned: "47-page comprehensive guide" signals depth without overwhelming
- Preview pages (3-5 pages shown in listing) are the #1 conversion driver -- choose visually rich pages
- Keywords: "[niche] guide", "[topic] ebook", "[skill] handbook", "how to [outcome] PDF"
- Printable formatting option increases appeal: "Designed for both screen reading and printing"
- Bundle strategy: starter guide + advanced guide + templates pack as a discounted bundle
