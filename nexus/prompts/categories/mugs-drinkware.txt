Category: POD Mugs & Drinkware
Specific rules:
- Mugs are the #1 gift item in POD -- always include gift-giving language and occasions
- Design must work on a curved surface -- avoid designs that rely on being viewed perfectly flat
- Wrap-around designs that use the full mug surface command premium pricing over single-side prints
- Text-heavy designs (quotes, job titles, hobby references) outperform purely illustrative designs
- Price range: $14-$22 for standard mugs, $18-$28 for color-changing or oversized, $24-$35 for premium ceramic
- Keywords: "[profession] mug", "funny [hobby] coffee mug", "[identity] gift mug", "custom [niche] cup"
- Mockup showing the mug with steam/coffee creates an emotional lifestyle appeal
- Dishwasher and microwave safe claims are expected -- always mention in product details
- Handle color options (black, red, blue interior) create variations from a single design
- Morning routine connection: "Start your day right" messaging resonates with coffee mug buyers
- Seasonal gifting peaks: Christmas, Father's Day, Mother's Day, Boss's Day, graduation season
