Category: Website Templates & Themes
Specific rules:
- Buyers want a professional online presence without hiring a developer -- sell the transformation
- Platform specificity is essential: WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, Squarespace, HTML/CSS -- never mix
- Mobile responsiveness is non-negotiable -- mention "fully responsive" and show mobile mockups
- Price range: $19-$49 for landing pages, $49-$99 for multi-page themes, $99-$249 for complete site kits
- Keywords: "[platform] theme", "[niche] website template", "[industry] landing page", "[platform] starter template"
- Live demo site is the #1 conversion driver -- buyers must see the template working before purchase
- Page speed and Core Web Vitals scores are increasingly important selling points
- Include: multiple page layouts, header/footer variations, blog templates, contact forms
- Setup documentation with step-by-step instructions reduces support requests and negative reviews
- SEO-ready structure (proper heading hierarchy, meta tags, schema markup) is a premium differentiator
- Niche-specific templates (restaurant, portfolio, SaaS, agency) outperform generic multi-purpose themes
- License terms: specify single site vs. unlimited sites, personal vs. commercial use
