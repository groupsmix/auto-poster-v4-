Category: Checklists & Trackers
Specific rules:
- Buyers want structure to avoid forgetting steps or losing progress -- sell peace of mind and completeness
- Actionable, sequential steps are the core value -- every item must be specific and immediately doable
- Visual progress indicators (checkboxes, progress bars, completion percentages) increase engagement
- Price range: $3-$9 for single checklists, $12-$27 for tracker bundles, $29-$49 for complete systems
- Printable + digital versions in one package increase perceived value with minimal extra effort
- Keywords: "[process] checklist", "[goal] tracker", "[niche] planner", "habit tracker", "[project] template"
- Niche specificity wins: "Wedding Day Timeline Checklist" outsells "Event Planning Checklist" 5:1
- Include space for notes, dates, and custom entries -- rigid templates feel limiting
- Habit trackers and goal trackers benefit from 30/60/90-day timeframes with built-in review points
- Pair with a guide or tips section explaining WHY each checklist item matters
- Color-coded categories and sections make complex checklists scannable and less overwhelming
