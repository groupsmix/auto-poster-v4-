STEP 2 — SCORE EACH CRITERION:

1. TITLE STRENGTH (1-10): Does this title STOP scrolling and DEMAND a click?
   - Does it front-load the primary keyword in the first 40 characters?
   - Is it within the platform's character limit?
   - Would it stand out in a grid of 20 competitor titles?
   - Does it speak to the target buyer's identity or pain point?
   - Does it avoid generic AI-style titles? ("Ultimate Guide to..." = instant fail)
   Example of a 9/10 title: "ADHD Planner Notion Template | Daily Focus Dashboard + Weekly Review System"
   Example of a 4/10 title: "The Ultimate Comprehensive Life Organization System Template"

2. DESCRIPTION QUALITY (1-10): Would a real buyer read this and click "Add to Cart"?
   - Does the FIRST sentence hook the reader with a pain point or vivid scenario?
   - Does it lead with benefits and transformations, not feature lists?
   - Is the formatting scannable (bullets, headers, short paragraphs)?
   - Does it address: who it's for, what they get, what problem it solves, and why THIS one?
   - Does it include social proof or credibility signals?
   - Is there a clear, specific call to action (not just "Buy now")?
   - Is it the right length? (Too short = not convincing. Too long = no one reads it.)

3. SEO QUALITY (1-10): Will the algorithm FIND and RANK this listing?
   - Is the primary keyword in the title, first sentence of description, and at least 3 tags?
   - Are all available tag/keyword slots used? (Etsy = 13 tags, Redbubble = 15)
   - Do tags mix broad terms with specific long-tail phrases?
   - Do tags avoid repeating exact words already in the title?
   - Are keywords buyer-intent focused? ("buy notion planner" not "what is notion")

4. PRICE LOGIC (1-10): Is the price optimized for maximum revenue?
   - Is it within the competitive range but not the cheapest? (Cheapest = low perceived value)
   - Does the description justify the price with clear value?
   - Is psychological pricing used ($12.99 not $13, $27 not $30)?
   - Are upsell or bundle opportunities mentioned where appropriate?
   - Does the price match the quality signals in the listing?

5. PLATFORM FIT (1-10): Would a platform expert approve this for THEIR audience?
   - Is the tone calibrated for this platform's specific buyer psychology?
   - Are ALL platform-specific formatting rules followed (char limits, tag counts, etc.)?
   - Does it use the platform's preferred content structure?
   - Are platform-specific trust signals included (reviews, guarantees, shipping info)?

6. HUMAN QUALITY (1-10): Could an AI detector tell this was AI-generated?
   - Scan for these instant-fail AI patterns: "elevate", "unlock", "game-changer", "delve", "seamless", "comprehensive", "In today's...", "Whether you're..."
   - Does sentence length VARY naturally? (Short. Medium length. Then a longer one that flows more conversationally.)
   - Are there contractions (don't, won't, it's) that sound natural?
   - Does it feel like a real expert human wrote this, not a language model?
   - Would it pass an AI detection tool? If any paragraph would fail, score 6 or below.

7. OVERALL READINESS (1-10): If you published this RIGHT NOW, would you be proud?
   - Would you publish this under your own brand with your name on it?
   - Are all required fields complete, consistent, and error-free?
   - Is there anything that would embarrass you if a top competitor saw it?
   - Does every element (title, description, tags, price, images) work together cohesively?
   - Is this in the top 15% of listings in this category? (Score 8 = yes)
