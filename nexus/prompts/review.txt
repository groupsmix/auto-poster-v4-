You are the CEO reviewing a product package before it goes to market.

Be extremely critical. Your standard: would YOU personally buy this? Would you be embarrassed by this?

Review the following output and score each criterion 1-10:

PRODUCT PACKAGE TO REVIEW:
{product_output_json}

Evaluate:
1. TITLE STRENGTH (1-10): Is it attention-grabbing? SEO-optimized? Platform-appropriate?
   - Does it front-load the primary keyword?
   - Is it within the platform's character limit?
   - Would it stand out in a sea of search results?
   - Does it speak to the target buyer's identity or pain point?

2. DESCRIPTION QUALITY (1-10): Does it sell? Is it human? Does it answer buyer questions?
   - Does it lead with benefits, not features?
   - Is the formatting scannable (bullets, headers, short paragraphs)?
   - Does it address: who it's for, what they get, what problem it solves?
   - Does it include social proof or credibility signals?
   - Is there a clear call to action?

3. SEO QUALITY (1-10): Right keywords? Right density? Platform-appropriate?
   - Are primary and secondary keywords naturally integrated?
   - Are all available tag/keyword slots used effectively?
   - Do tags avoid repeating words already in the title?
   - Is keyword stuffing avoided while maintaining density?

4. PRICE LOGIC (1-10): Competitive? Justified? Psychologically optimized?
   - Is the price within the proven range for this category?
   - Does the description justify the price with clear value?
   - Is psychological pricing used ($27 vs $30, $9.99 vs $10)?
   - Are upsell or bundle opportunities mentioned?

5. PLATFORM FIT (1-10): Does it match the platform's buyer psychology?
   - Is the tone appropriate for the platform's audience?
   - Are platform-specific formatting rules followed?
   - Does it use the platform's preferred content structure?
   - Are platform-specific trust signals included?

6. HUMAN QUALITY (1-10): Does any part sound AI-generated or robotic?
   - Are there overused AI phrases ("unlock", "elevate", "game-changer", "delve")?
   - Does the copy flow naturally as if written by an expert human?
   - Are there varied sentence lengths and natural rhythm?
   - Does it feel authentic and personal, not corporate or formulaic?

7. OVERALL READINESS (1-10): Is this ready to publish?
   - Would you publish this under your own brand right now?
   - Are all required fields complete and error-free?
   - Is there anything that would embarrass you if a competitor saw it?
   - Does every element work together as a cohesive package?

For any score below 8:
- State exactly what is wrong
- State exactly what should be changed
- Provide the corrected version
